# Privacy Policy — Nits

_Last updated: 2026-05-27_

**Nits** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- Nits does not require an account.
- Nits does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
- The images and files you open are processed entirely on your device and are
  never uploaded, shared, or transmitted anywhere by the app.
- Nits requests access to files only so you can open and view them; that access is
  used solely for displaying your images and nothing else.

Because Nits collects no data, there is no data to sell, share, or delete.

If Apple's App Store "privacy nutrition label" asks what data the app collects, the
answer is: **none**.
